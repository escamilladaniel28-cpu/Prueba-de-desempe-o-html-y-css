Who We Are

About the Band

Shattered Riffs is a high-energy rock band born in the underground scene of Madrid. Known for their powerful live performances and raw, unfiltered sound, they have been shaking stages across Europe and Latin America since 2019.

With two studio albums and over 150 live shows, Shattered Riffs has built a loyal fanbase that lives and breathes rock music.

Our Influences

  • Classic Rock — Led Zeppelin, AC/DC
  • Hard Rock — Guns N' Roses, Aerosmith
  • Alternative — Foo Fighters, Pearl Jam
  • Metal — Metallica, Black Sabbath
